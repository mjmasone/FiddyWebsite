How it works

Simple to organize.
Easy to play.

Built to run anywhere, at any game, by anyone.

01 — Set up

Create your raffle

Log in, name your raffle, set a ticket price, and add your Venmo handle. You're live in under five minutes.

02 — Share

Distribute your link or flyer

Share a link, text a QR code, or print a flyer. Players buy tickets from any phone by scanning or clicking a link — no account, no download.

03 — Draw

Pick a winner, pay the winner

When you're ready, draw a winner with one tap. The system walks you through the payout back to the winner. Done.
